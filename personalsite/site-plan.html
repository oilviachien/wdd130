Content

Home page

Simple Structure of Home page: I will show my audience what kind of food our website will provide. I put three categories rice, noodles, and snakes. And I will put pictures for each country with correlated food categories. For example, I will put Taiwanese, Japanese, and Korean rice dishes’ pictures in the rice section, and so does noodles and snakes. (I will put my wireframe down the page, that would be easier to understand.) I would like to put the link in each picture on this page so that if my audiences are interested in any of them they can click it and find the recipe. Also, I think for a food recipe website, putting a lot of pictures would be more attractive.

Images for the Home page

Taiwanese Food

For this page, I would like to have the dishes’ names, ingredients, and a video to show people how to make them. I would like to have a main meal (ex, rice and noodles), and snakes section.
